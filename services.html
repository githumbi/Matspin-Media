Services

We Provide the following services:

MATSPIN MEDIA AD's

Riding on the content played on matatu screens, MATSPIN MEDIA embed advertisements within the content to deliver messaging in spaces where consumers are.

The Content is displayed in the Matatu Screens in between music mixes and other interesting contents.

Using our inhouse online playout software, the content is curated and played in real-time in all the active locations.

MATSPIN MEDIA Graffiti Branding

MATSPIN MEDIA Partnership with Matatu SACCOs also entails full access of their affiliated matatus for branding options.

We will infuse graffiti with brands to create unconventional way of branding that is not tiring to the eyes.

MATSPIN MEDIA Social WiFi

MATSPIN MEDIA Social WiFi is offered free of charge in Matatus, Restaurants and entertainment joints across the country.
