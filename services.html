Services

We Provide the following services:

MATSPIN MEDIA AD's

The matatu culture boasts its richness in ability to actively engage commuters through music and video strategically placed in their daily commute. Matspin media skillfully rides on this option by embedding adverts on this content ensuring brand delivery in spaces where consumers are. Using our in-house online playout software, the content is curated and played in real time in all active locations.

MATSPIN MEDIA Graffiti Branding

MATSPIN MEDIA Partnership with Matatu SACCOs also entails full access of their affiliated matatus for branding options.

We will infuse graffiti with brands to create unconventional way of branding that is not tiring to the eyes.

MATSPIN MEDIA Social WiFi

We offer free wifi on the go within matatus, restaurants and entertainment joints across the country. Once our users get connected, the spin wifi serves them with Ads and also does data collection as clients wish; in some instances we customize a call to action exposure to your needs.

MARKET SCOOP

We specialize in a dynamic call to market campaigns that involve interactive activations in matatus plying within the route of your business, ensuring your product promotion maximum flow while giving the local audiences ownership of your brand. We create unforgettable experiences through information and applying direct active engagement with the consumer.
